Who We Are

We are EnviroMenders, an organization that is committed to combating the climate crisis. EnviroMenders users can participate in small activities that will help the environment. Once they reach a certain number of points, they are eligible to receive a prize from our corporate sponsors.

Mission Statement

We are trying to fix the world that we broke. We are trying to mend the environment.
